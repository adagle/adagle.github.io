About Us
​The Temps is a Senior Design group consisting of five students in the Mechanical Engineering department at Columbia University. As part of our senior design course, we decided to create a hexapedal robot with the intent of aiding with surveillance. Through out the one year course, we implemented components used in previous hexapedal robots and focused on the robot's ability to transverse a wide variety of terrain.
