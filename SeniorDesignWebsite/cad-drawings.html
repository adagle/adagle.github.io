ABOUT ME

Our Design
PACO is just like any ordinary boy, conceptualized by a team of engineers with SolidWorks in their hands.  Alright, we may not have paid enough attention during biology...but our engineering skills have been refined over the years. Over the course of several redesigns, PACO took his final form. A motor mount was designed to minimize the torque on the motor shaft through a series of bearings and a timing belt to offset the motor shaft from the leg shaft. The shape and dimensions of the C-shaped legs were fine tuned. Electronic components were precisely planned to fit securely in place atop an aluminum plate which forms the chassis of our robot. After the parts were carefully assembled in Solidworks, engineering drawings were created and our machinists got to work bringing PACO to life.
